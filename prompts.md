# Initial

Let's build an application to capture fuel consumption over time. It should allow me to enter a record for each vehicle I drive. A vehicle record should include the make, model, the starting odometer reading and year the vehicle was produced. When I visit a gas station I will keep a receipt. When entering a receipt I would like to select a vehicle, enter the volume of fuel, the price per litre, the date of the receipt, the odometer reading and the name of the gas station. After entering a receipt, I need to find the most recent odometer reading for the vehicle to subtract from the current reading to calculate the distance travelled to then calculate the consumption (expressed as l/100km).
