About Gas Log

Version 1.0.0

Track your vehicle's fuel consumption and calculate L/100km.

How consumption is calculated:

(Fuel Volume in Litres ÷ Distance Travelled in km) × 100 = L/100km

Data Storage:

All data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server. Use the Export button to back up your data.

Tips for accurate tracking:

  • Fill up the tank completely each time
  • Enter the odometer reading from your vehicle
  • Record receipts immediately after filling up

Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Works offline.
